About Me

Hello! I am a mechanical engineer from Dhaka, Bangladesh.

I completed my undergrad from Department of Mechanical Engineering, BUET, on February, 2020. My undergrad thesis was about designing wind turbines for low wind speed regions. However, since late 2015, I got interested into capital market and finance. Later on interest for business analytics grew in me.

I pass my time watching movies (I am a cinephile), listing to music (my favorite genre is classic pop melodies) and reading about anything. I love to travel and used to be a photographer.

Experience
